Dart and Flutter Developer Relations (DevRel) grows and supports the Dart and Flutter developer communities. We onboard and retain developers by building a fun, smart, and approachable culture, providing resources to help them succeed, and advocating on their behalf.

As a Technical Writer for Flutter, you'll join a community of developers using Dart and Flutter to build apps for mobile, web, and desktop.

Important: This job requires work authorization in the United States.

Job responsibilities

  • Write documents, blog posts, and technical guides for Flutter.
  • Take responsibility for individual content areas, plan their information architecture, and build documents to match.
  • Interview engineers, product managers, and others to gather technical details for documents.
  • Edit writing from internal and external contributors at a variety of proficiency levels and help them grow.
  • Publish content using a variety of media and tools.

Job location

  • Seattle, WA
  • Portland, OR
  • San Francisco, CA
  • Mountain View, CA
  • Remote within the U.S.

Minimum qualifications

You must meet these minimum qualifications to apply for this job:

  • Ability to work in the U.S.
  • 4 years of experience creating content for a technical audience (e.g., developer documentation, computer science course materials, and/or IT admin playbooks).
  • Experience with software or hardware systems working on investigation/experimentation (e.g. reading or running code), and/or interactions with Subject Matter Experts.

Preferred qualifications

Having these qualifications is a plus, but transferable skills/experiences may be equally valuable:

  • Experience with advocacy for under-served developer populations.
  • Experience contributing to open source documentation.
  • Experience working cross-functionally with engineers, product managers, developers, and more.
